My Trips

Maldives

  • Why Maldives? The Maldives is a tropical holiday destination
    in the Indian Ocean. The stunning islands are very relaxed and
    offer an experience which can best be described as a getaway on
    a desert island but with high quality accommodation, spectacular
    food, excellent service and an extensive range of activities.

Japan

  • Why Japan? Japan is one of the oldest civilizations and has a
    beautiful and diverse history. The stunning, diverse scenery with
    mountains and breathtaking views, which are much appreciated
    by the Japanese, offers so many different experiences that attract
    tourists from all corners of the world.
